Viator Engineering Open Roles

Viator connects suppliers to travelers, creating the world's largest platform for travel experiences.

We are growing fast and have many positions to fill in Portugal and the UK. We are looking for engineers at all levels for full-stack, backend and data teams.

Roles in Portugal require you to be in the office in Lisbon part time. UK roles can be part time in the office in London or Oxford or fully remote. For the remote roles you still need to be based somewhere in the UK.

If you see more than one listing that appeals to you, apply for one role only and then discuss your options during the recruitment process. The recruiters will be happy to help find the best fit for you. All roles are handled by a single recruitment team so there is no benefit in sending multiple applications.
